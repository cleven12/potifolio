About TechTour

What Is This?

TechTour (also called TechTravel) is my ongoing project to document and share insights from the intersection of software engineering and Tanzania's tourism industry. I build systems in this space — booking platforms, open APIs, AI chatbots — and I write about what I learn along the way.

If you work in tourism tech, are a developer curious about African markets, or just want to understand how software shapes travel experiences in East Africa — this space is for you.
