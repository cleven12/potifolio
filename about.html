About Me

Who I Am

CS student, freelance backend & AI engineer, open-source contributor — building from Kilimanjaro Region, Tanzania.

The Person

I'm Cleven Godson, a self-driven Computer Science student at Mwenge Catholic University (MWECAU) in Tanzania. I've been freelancing since 2023, building backend systems, REST APIs, and AI-powered tools for clients worldwide — while staying rooted in my corner of East Africa.

I believe software should solve real problems for real people — especially in underserved markets like Tanzania's tourism sector and small-to-medium businesses. I'm drawn to the intersection of AI, APIs, and practical product engineering.
